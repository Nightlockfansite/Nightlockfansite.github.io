Hello and welcome to this fansite!  This site will contain facts about Panem and the people who live there, news about the fandom, quizzes and much, much more.   This site contain spoilers, so I would recommend you to read the books before you explore this website. I hope you'll enjoy it.

The Hunger Games is a book serie written by Suzanne Collins. The three books are called 'The Hunger Games', 'Catching Fire', and 'Mockingjay'. The first book was published in 2008, and the first movie was released in 2012. Both 'The Hunger Games' and 'Catching Fire' were New York Times Bestsellers, and 'Mockingjay' topped all US Bestsellers upon its release. I hope you enjoyed the books and films just as much as i did!
